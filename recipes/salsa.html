Salsa

Description

Here's another great use for fresh tomatoes in the summer. This is even better the next day, once the flavors have had time to develop.

Ingredients

  • 1/2 of a red onion, coarsely chopped
  • 1 tbsp minced garlic
  • 1 lb tomatoes (Roma recommended)
  • 1 jalapeno pepper, chopped with seeds removed
  • handful of fresh cilantro
  • juice and zest of 1 lime
  • 1/2 tsp sea salt
  • 1/4 tsp ground cumin
  • pinch sugar

Steps

  1. In a food processor, combine the onion and garlic until well-chopped.
  2. Add all the other ingredients to the food processor and pulse until combined but still chunky.
  3. If desired, strain out some of the water.
  4. Chill for a few hours or overnight in fridge. Serve with chips.
