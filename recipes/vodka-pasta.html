Penne Alla Vodka

Description

This is my sister, Ella's, recipe for vodka sauce. Fresh tomatoes are always good, but whole canned tomatoes are great too.

Ingredients

  • 1 tbsp olive oil
  • 1 large onion, diced
  • 1 tbsp minced garlic
  • dried basil
  • red pepper flakes
  • 1/3 cup vodka
  • 1 can whole tomatoes (or 1 lb fresh)
  • 1/4 cup heavy whipping cream
  • penne pasta, cooked
  • Parmesan cheese

Steps

  1. Add olive oil to pan over medium heat.
  2. Add diced onion and saute until it starts to brown.
  3. Add garlic to pan. After 30 seconds, add basil and red pepper flakes as well as salt and pepper.
  4. Pour vodka into pan. Stir frequently until the liquid is mostly cooked away.
  5. Add the tomatoes. Crush them with spoon or spatula as they cook. Stir frequently.
  6. Once the moisture has mostly cooked off, add cream until the sauce is a pale orange.
  7. Serve over pasta, topped with grated Parmesan.
