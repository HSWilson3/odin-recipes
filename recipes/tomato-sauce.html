Tomato Sauce

Description

This is my mom's recipe for tomato sauce. She likes it with cherry tomatoes, but I think they have too many seeds.

Ingredients

  • 1/4 cup olive oil
  • 2 pints cherry tomatoes, or 1 lb larger ones
  • 1 large onion, diced
  • 2 tbsp minced garlic
  • salt
  • pepper
  • red pepper flakes
  • spaghetti, cooked
  • fresh basil
  • Parmesan cheese

Steps

  1. Add olive oil to pan over medium heat.
  2. Add diced onion and saute until it starts to brown.
  3. Add tomatoes to pan and get them coated in oil.
  4. After about 5 minutes, when they are getting somewhat soft, add salt and red pepper to taste, along with the garlic.
  5. Cook for another 10 minutes, stirring often.
  6. Use a wooden spoon or potato masher to crush the tomatoes. They should be saucy, but still chunky.
  7. Serve over pasta, topped with shredded basil and grated Parmesan.
